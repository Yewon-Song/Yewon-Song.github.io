About

Hello! I am a first-year M.S. student in Automotive Engineering at Hanyang University, advised by Prof. Soonmin Hwang at IRCV Lab. My research focuses on LiDAR semantic segmentation and test-time adaptation for autonomous driving.

Before Hanyang University, I completed my B.S. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Konkuk University (2020 – 2025). I also gained industry experience as an AI Research Intern at SkyAutoNet and DCVL.
